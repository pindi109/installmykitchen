## Your Job
Read the actual built HTML pages in `dist/` for your assigned category.
For each page, assess:

1. **Content depth** — Is there enough substantive text, or is it thin/templated?
2. **Uniqueness** — Does this page say something different from other pages of the same type, or is it near-identical boilerplate with only the location/brand swapped?
3. **Intent match** — Does the content answer what someone searching for this page would actually want?
4. **Missing content** — What topics, FAQs, stats, trust signals, or sections are obviously absent?
5. **Strongest pages** — Which 2-3 pages in your batch are genuinely good?
6. **Weakest pages** — Which 2-3 are the thinnest/most templated?
7. **Scalable fix** — What ONE change to the template would improve all pages of this type?

## Report Format
Return a structured report:
- Category name and page count reviewed
- Overall content quality score: 1-10
- Strongest pages (with reason)
- Weakest pages (with reason)
- Top 3 specific improvement opportunities (concrete, actionable)
- Template-level fix recommendation
- Any critical SEO issues noticed
